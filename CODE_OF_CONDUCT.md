# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the format
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments in any form
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling or sustained disruptive behavior in issues or PRs

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other venue where this project is represented.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainer at **km.vibecoder@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality.

Violations may result in a warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the project at the maintainer's discretion.
