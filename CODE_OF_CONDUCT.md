# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a beneficial experience focused on growth and development with dignity and respect for every person.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Communicating in ways that treat others with dignity and respect
- Valuing input from all contributors to solve problems effectively
- Welcoming contributors at all experience levels with an eagerness to teach, learn, and grow together
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Seeking the benefit of the community and the format in all decisions
- Showing kindness to help ourselves and others become better contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments in any form
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling or sustained disruptive behavior in issues or PRs

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other venue where this project is represented.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported via [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/km-vibecoder/MusicalMarkdown/discussions). All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality.

Violations may result in a warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the project at the maintainer's discretion.
